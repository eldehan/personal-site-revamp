Antiquitas Interactive is a web application that leverages artificial intelligence to let users engage in conversations with historical figures from the ancient Mediterranean world. Users can ask questions, learn from historical personas, and explore insights from primary sources.

Powered by OpenAI's GPT technology, the project aims to bring historical education to life using AI-powered personas. It's being built with a modern stack including React, Node.js, and MongoDB.
